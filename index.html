What is AskAling?

AskAling AskAling is your friendly barangay chatbot that helps residents easily find answers about local services and community concerns. Inspired by the Filipino term “Aling”—often used to address a familiar and approachable neighbor—the chatbot feels like talking to someone you know and trust. Whether it’s about barangay clearances, schedules, or programs, AskAling is here to provide quick and reliable information, just like asking a helpful neighbor.
