About AskAling

What is AskAling?

AskAling is your friendly barangay chatbot that helps residents easily find answers about local services and community concerns. Inspired by the Filipino term “Aling”—often used to address a familiar and approachable neighbor—the chatbot feels like talking to someone you know and trust. Whether it’s about barangay clearances, schedules, or programs, AskAling is here to provide quick and reliable information, just like asking a helpful neighbor.

The Problem We Solve

  • Barangay residents often experience confusion and delays when looking for information like clearances, garbage schedules, health services, etc.
  • Barangay staff spend a lot of time answering repetitive inquiries.
  • Barangay information is often scattered across posters, social media posts, or word-of-mouth, making it inconsistent.
With AskAling, residents get reliable answers instantly while barangay staff save time for more important tasks.

Benefits of AskAling

For Barangay Staff

  • Reduces repetitive tasks.
  • Lets staff focus on more important governance work.
  • Improves overall efficiency of service delivery.

For Residents

  • Get clear answers instantly.
  • No need for repeated visits or long waits.
  • Accessible anytime, in Tagalog/Taglish.
AskAling is not about replacing human service
— it’s about enhancing it.
